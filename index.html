Overview

Project Title: Early Warning System

We made a system namely SHELTER to detect weather and potential disasters, there are forest fire, landslide, flood, and earthquake. This system is able to forecast those things upon one year from now, also as a platform to report it straight away.

Background

  • In fact, there was an increase in the area of forest fires in Indonesia by 376% between 2016 until 2019 (10.1109/IC3INA48034.2019.8949588)

  • The flood submerged the houses inhabited by 1,983 families or 5,965 people scattered in seven sub-districts in Medan City (Ministry of Public Works and Public Housing)

  • However, Indonesian people still have low awareness of preparing themselves for disasters (LIPI, CNN)

  • It is still difficult for residents to contact BPBD quickly so that many are evacuated late
